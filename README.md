# Trade_data_Analysis
This repository contains projects focused on the exploration, analysis, and economic interpretation of trade data.

## Overview

The purpose of this repository is to analyze domestic and international trade datasets in order to extract meaningful insights related to trade flows, market structure, competitiveness, and economic trends. The work combines data exploration techniques with applied economic analysis.

## Key Areas of Focus
Projects in this repository may include:

1. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

2 . Trade volumes and values

3. Import/export trends

4. Country and regional trade patterns

5. Product and sector-level breakdowns

6. Trade & Economic Analysis

7. Comparative advantage and specialization

8. Market concentration and diversification

9. Trade balance and growth dynamics


## Data Sources

1. Publicly available trade datasets - BACI 

2. International trade databases (e.g., UN Comtrade, World Bank, WTO)

## Methods & Tools

1. Python (pandas, numpy, matplotlib, seaborn)

2. Statistical and econometric techniques

3. Time-series and cross-sectional analysis

4. Data cleaning and normalization workflows

## Intended Audience

This repository is intended for:

1. Economic and trade research

2. Data analysis and modeling practice

3. Policy and market intelligence exploration

4. Academic and professional portfolio work
